Safty Tips For
After the Initial 500 Miles...
  1. Change Engine Oil.
  2. Inspect oil lines and
    Brake System for Leaks.
  3. Check Operation of
    Electrical Equipment & Switches.
  4. Change Transmission Lubricant & Clean
    Magnetic Drain Plug.
  5. Check Rear Shock Absorbers.
  6. Check Wheel Spoke Tightness.
  7. Check Rear Fork Pivot Nut.
